# Privacy Policy

**Lock** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- No analytics. No tracking. No ads. No network calls.
- No user accounts.
- No data ever leaves your device.

The Accessibility permission is used solely to lock the screen when you tap the app icon, widget, or tile. It is not used to read window content or observe any app.

Source code: https://github.com/RealQuickLtd/lock

## Children's Privacy

The app is safe for all ages. It does not collect any information from anyone, including children.
